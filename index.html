Why this matters

Most small businesses do not need more fear. They need a clear first move.

Cyber risk becomes expensive when it is unclear, ignored or left entirely to chance. A short, practical review helps you understand what is visible, what could be abused and which fixes are worth doing first.

MTC Cyber is designed for business owners who want sensible security progress — not an overwhelming audit or a pile of tools they do not have time to manage.

Common questions

Good questions before you start

Will this affect my website or customer data?

No. The Snapshot starts with non-intrusive public-facing checks only. We do not log in, bypass controls or access customer data.

What if I already have an IT provider?

Great. The report is designed to make that relationship more productive by giving your provider a clear, prioritised list of items to confirm or fix.

Is this only for technical businesses?

No. The service is specifically built for cafes, trades, mechanics, local services and other owner-operated businesses that need practical guidance.

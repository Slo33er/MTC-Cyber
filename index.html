Common questions

Short, honest answers to what most small business owners ask.

Will this scan take my website offline or touch customer data?

No. The Cyber Readiness Snapshot uses non-intrusive checks against your public-facing website and DNS only. We do not attempt to log in, bypass controls or access customer data.

Do I need an IT team already?

No. The report is written so that you can understand it yourself. If you have an IT provider, you can simply send them the findings and recommended fixes.

How long does the snapshot take?

Once confirmed, most snapshots are turned around within a short timeframe (depending on workload and complexity). You will receive your report by email as a PDF.

What does it cost?

The basic Cyber Readiness Snapshot is offered free or at a very low cost for genuine small businesses, as an awareness and relationship-building service. Deeper reviews and ongoing advisory are quoted case-by-case, with clear pricing up front.

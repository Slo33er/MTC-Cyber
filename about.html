About MTC Cyber

Practical cyber security for the businesses that keep Australia running day-to-day.

From containers to cyber

MTC Cyber was founded by Matthew Constantinou in Victoria after more than a decade working in the shipping container and logistics industry. Leading teams on the ground showed just how vulnerable everyday businesses can be when it comes to technology and online risk.

Instead of focusing only on big-budget enterprise environments, MTC Cyber is built to serve the other side of the economy: cafes, mechanics, trades, local services, small online stores and family-run operators.

The approach is simple:

  • Explain cyber risks in plain English, not confusing jargon.
  • Focus on what actually reduces risk in the real world.
  • Respect your time, budget and how your business operates.

What MTC Cyber believes in

Plain language

Cyber security should not require a dictionary. Every report, recommendation and call aims to be as simple and direct as possible.

Ethical, non-intrusive checks

All assessments are performed with your permission and start with public-facing information only. You remain in control of your systems and data.

Realistic improvements

Suggestions are tailored to the tools and people you already have, not to an imaginary enterprise environment.

Local focus

Based in Wallan, Victoria and focused on helping Australian small businesses strengthen their resilience to modern cyber threats.

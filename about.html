Who We Are

At MTC Cyber, we believe that every small business deserves enterprise-level protection — without the enterprise-level price tag.

Founded by Matthew Constantinou, a qualified Cyber Defence Analyst with over a decade of hands-on experience in logistics and operations, MTC Cyber was built to make cybersecurity simple, transparent, and practical for everyday businesses.

Matthew understands that most cyber threats don’t come with warning signs. That’s why he created the Cyber Readiness Snapshot — a quick, non-intrusive assessment that highlights risks before attackers do. From missing security headers to exposed ports and weak TLS settings, MTC Cyber pinpoints vulnerabilities in plain English and provides clear next steps to strengthen your digital resilience.

Our Mission

To make cybersecurity accessible and actionable for small businesses — so you can focus on running your business, not defending it.

What We Do

  • Free Cyber Readiness Snapshot scans for websites
  • Clear, jargon-free security reports and next-step advice
  • Affordable fixes and hardening plans for small business websites
  • Ongoing monitoring and education to maintain strong defences

Local Focus

Based in Wallan, Victoria, MTC Cyber focuses on helping local businesses improve their security posture, meet compliance expectations, and build customer trust. Whether you run a mechanic shop, café, or community organisation, we can help you stay protected, confident, and prepared.

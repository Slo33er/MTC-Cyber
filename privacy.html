Privacy Policy

How MTC Cyber handles your information.

1. Overview

MTC Cyber respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information that we collect in connection with our website and services.

2. What we collect

When you contact us or request a Cyber Readiness Snapshot, we may collect:

  • Your name and contact details (such as email and phone number).
  • Your business name and website/domain details.
  • Information you choose to include in your message.

3. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries.
  • Provide the requested snapshot or other services.
  • Maintain records of work performed.

4. Storage and security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect the information we hold from misuse, loss and unauthorised access. Access is limited to those who need it to provide services.

5. Third-party services

We may use third-party services (for example, email providers or form processing tools) to help us operate the website and respond to you. These services may store or process your information on our behalf.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or how your information is handled, contact:
